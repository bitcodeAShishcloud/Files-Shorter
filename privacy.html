1. Overview

Secure File Upload is a personal, non-commercial web application that allows users to upload files directly to their own Google Drive account, organized by subject-based folders. This application runs entirely in your browser and does not store any user data on third-party servers.

2. Data We Access

This application requests the following Google API scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file - View and manage Google Drive files and folders that you have opened or created with this app

What this means:

  • ✅ The app can create new files/folders in your Drive
  • ✅ The app can manage only files it created
  • ❌ The app CANNOT access your existing Drive files
  • ❌ The app CANNOT share, delete, or modify files outside its own creations

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • File Uploads: Files are uploaded directly from your browser to YOUR Google Drive using Google's official API. No file content passes through or is stored on any server controlled by the developer.
  • Your Name: The name you enter in the form is only used to prefix uploaded filenames (e.g., "Ashish_notes.pdf"). It is not stored, logged, or shared.
  • Subject Categories: Subject names are used only to organize folders in your Drive. Custom subjects are saved only in your browser's local storage.
  • Authentication: Google handles all authentication. The app receives only an access token with limited scope, never your password.

4. Data Storage & Retention

We do not store:

  • ❌ Your uploaded files
  • ❌ Your Google account credentials
  • ❌ Your personal information
  • ❌ Usage logs or analytics

All data remains in your personal Google Drive account under your control.

5. Your Rights & Controls

  • Delete Files: You can delete any uploaded file directly from your Google Drive at any time.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the app's access to your account anytime: Google Account Permissions
  • Stop Using: You may stop using the app at any time with no penalty.
  • Request Data: Since we store no user data, there is no data to request or export.

6. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • Google Drive API: Subject to Google API Services User Data Policy
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting service, subject to GitHub Terms of Service
